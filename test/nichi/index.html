Nichi is a pure survival server. Since Minecraft survival is inherently ruleless, we find it redundant and unproductive to brand the server as "Anarchy".

Instead, Nichi's aim as a server is to challenge the current notion of what a "no rules" survival server looks like. A breath of fresh air from the heavily performative, 'hack-centric', toxic culture of modern Anarchy servers, Nichi takes influence from the culture of older, "freebuild" survival servers.

Like the long-gone Civcraft, Nichi is
 "an experiment for communities, political ideologies, debate and discussion. Forming a new direction for gameplay, not just about surviving the elements, but surviving each other, where players can come together to create civilization or to watch it crumble. A world open to any idea, manifesto, or philosophy, created by the players. You build this world, but in doing so, you take on the responsibilities of protecting it. This is the great pastime and challenge of mankind: Civilization"
